Hand movement detection demo built with MediaPipe Hand Landmarker and webcam input.

How it works: each video frame is processed in-browser to extract 21 hand landmarks. Wrist displacement across consecutive frames is converted into motion speed (px/s), then classified as moving vs still in real time.

Camera is off. Click Start Camera to begin.
